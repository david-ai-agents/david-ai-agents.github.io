üéØ Section 2: Pick a Business Process to Transform

Identify the right process for maximum impact

Most executives we speak with have a few things in mind when we ask them - what process is currently costing you the most money?

Don't try to "fix everything." Instead, look for one process that fits into one of the three buckets below.

1. Revenue Generating Tasks (The "Unlock" Bucket)

Focus: Freeing up your best talent to generate new value. Think about where your most expensive or talented people are getting bogged down. If you could reclaim 20% of their week, could they close more deals, build better products, or serve more customers?

Ask yourself: "Where is my team doing 'busy work' that prevents them from selling or strategizing?"

Look for: Sales outreach, personalized marketing content, customer onboarding, or drafting proposals.

2. Operating Expenses (The "Efficiency" Bucket)

Focus: Reducing the cost of doing business. Identify the tasks that happen frequently, consume a lot of hours, and require little "human" judgment. These are the administrative burdens that bloat your overhead.

Ask yourself: "What is a necessary evil that we spend way too much time and money on?"

Look for: Invoice processing, scheduling, data entry, report generation, or answering Tier 1 support questions.

3. Error-Prone Tasks (The "Quality" Bucket)

Focus: Reducing risk and costly mistakes. Find the processes where "human error" is a constant worry. These are often complex routines that require high accuracy, strict compliance, or intense inspection.

Ask yourself: "Where do we constantly have to double-check work or fix mistakes after the fact?"

Look for: Contract review, compliance checks, transferring data between systems, or quality assurance inspections.
